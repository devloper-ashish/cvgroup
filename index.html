Popular Destinations

Sanchi Stupa

Sanchi is a Buddhist complex, famous for its Great Stupa, on a hilltop at Sanchi Town in Raisen District of the State of Madhya Pradesh,It is located, about 23 KM from Raisen town, district headquarter and 46 km north-east of Bhopal, capital of Madhya Pradesh.Its nucleus was a simple hemispherical brick structure built over the relics of the Buddha

Pachmarhi

Pachmarhi is a hill station in the Narmadapuram district of Madhya Pradesh state of central India. It has been the location of a cantonment since British Raj.It is widely known as Satpura ki Rani situated at the height of 1067 m in a valley of the Satpura Range in the Narmadapuram district.It is a part of the Satpura Biosphere Reserve.

Ujjain-Mahakal lok

old name Avantika is a city in Ujjain district of the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. It is the fifth-largest city in Madhya Pradesh by population and is the administrative centre of Ujjain district and Ujjain division. It is one of the Hindu pilgrimage centres of Sapta Puri famous for the Kumbh Mela held there every 12 years.
